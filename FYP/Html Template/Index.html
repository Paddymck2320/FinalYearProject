NFL Predictor

Final Year Predicton Project for the NFL Season of 2021

Predictions

This is a Brief paragraph on how the predictions Work.

I have two seperate python functions the first one is for predicting the weekly games in the nfl from week 1 right through to week 18 in the weekly predictions tab in the web page I have the predictions made are displayed in the tables for each week. The other python function uses data from recent years and also gives the predictions in a table form by displaying the probable winner of the superbowl in 2021.

Idea?

What made me think of the Idea for an NFL Predictor.

I got the Idea from looking at articles about 'super computers' predicting the results in soccer matches like the champions league , I then realised that I could Revolve my project around predictions of another sport in this case was the NFL. I webscraped the significant data for predictions and made my predictions through python and machine learning.
